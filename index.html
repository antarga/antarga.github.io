Senior Application Engineer and Data Scientist

I am an engineer and data scientist with a strong foundation in applied mathematics and physical sciences, working at the intersection of industry and academic research. My work spans the full data science lifecycle — from data acquisition and engineering through model development, deployment, and long-term monitoring in production environments. Currently, I apply machine learning and optical data analysis to semiconductor metrology at n&k Technology, while maintaining active ties to the research community through collaborative publications.

I thrive in multidisciplinary environments, having contributed to teams across multiple domains such as semiconductor engineering, biomedical informatics, and computational mathematics. I communicate technical findings to both specialist and non-specialist audiences, including delivering on-site training at customer sites. Working remotely with a US-based team across time zones has reinforced my ability to self-organize, adapt to dynamic requirements, and maintain consistent delivery across research and production workstreams. I am driven by the challenge of turning rigorous methodology into reliable, production-ready tools that solve real problems.
